# Product Requirements Document — Animal Shelter Website

## Project Name Ideas
- FureverFriends
- PawsUnited
- PawsAndClaws
- FurAndFound 

---

## Overview

A website dedicated to helping animals — primarily dogs and cats — receive more love, care, and forever homes by connecting them with the people who can make a difference.

---

## Target Audience

- **Kids** — engaging, approachable content that sparks empathy for animals
- **Adults** — actionable ways to adopt, volunteer, or donate
- **Seniors** — clear, readable design with simple navigation

Anyone willing to help spread awareness to others in their community.

---

## Problem Being Solved

Dogs and cats in shelters are not receiving enough love and care. This site exists to bridge the gap between animals in need and people who are ready to help — whether through adoption, fostering, or simply sharing the mission.

---

## Site Sections

### 1. Title & Introduction
A clear, welcoming hero section that immediately communicates the site's purpose and draws visitors in.

### 2. Meet the Pets
Profiles of dogs and cats available for adoption — photos, names, ages, and personalities to help visitors connect with individual animals, also real animals. 

### 3. Why Adoption Matters
Educational content explaining the importance of adopting from shelters over buying from breeders, including statistics and real impact stories.

### 4. Pet Care
Resources and tips for new and prospective pet owners — feeding, health, training, and building a bond with a newly adopted animal.

### 5. How It Works (Working Page)
A step-by-step guide to the adoption process so visitors know exactly what to expect from first visit to bringing a pet home.

### 6. Call to Action
A compelling final section encouraging visitors to adopt, volunteer, donate, or share the site — making it easy to take the next step.

### 7. Working page
A website that actually works and can really promote animal awareness to the world.
---

## Goals

- Increase awareness of shelter animals in need
- Drive adoption inquiries and applications
- Educate the public on responsible pet care
- Build a community of advocates who spread the mission
