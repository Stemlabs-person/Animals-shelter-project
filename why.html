Why Adoption Matters

The facts speak for themselves — adopting saves lives.

6.3M

Animals enter U.S. shelters every single year. That's one animal every 5 seconds.

920K

Animals are euthanized annually in shelters — most of them perfectly healthy and loving.

2 Lives

Every adoption saves two lives — the pet you take home, and the one who takes their spot in the shelter.

25%

Of dogs in shelters are purebreds. You don't have to go to a breeder to find your dream dog.

🌍 Help Spread Awareness

You don't have to adopt to help. Sharing this page, talking to a neighbor, or posting about shelter animals on social media can inspire someone else to act. Every voice counts — especially yours.
