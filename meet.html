Golden Retriever

Friendly Gentle Playful Easy to Train

Golden Retrievers are one of the most beloved family dogs for a reason. They are patient with children, kind to seniors, and get along with nearly everyone they meet. Most are already vaccinated and know basic commands when adopted from a shelter.

Ideal for: Families, first-time dog owners, seniors, kids of all ages
