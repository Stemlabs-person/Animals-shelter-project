Meet the Pets

These real shelter animals are looking for a forever home. Could yours be next?

Golden Retriever

Dog · Friendly · Great with kids & seniors

Golden Retrievers are gentle, playful, and love everyone they meet. They know basic commands and make wonderful family companions.

Adopt a Golden Retriever

Tabby

Cat · Affectionate · Great for apartments

Tabbies are curious, warm, and love sunny windowsills and lap cuddles. They are easygoing and great for calm households.

Adopt a Tabby

Beagle

Dog · Loyal · Good for all ages

Beagles are loyal, calm, and love daily walks and couch cuddles. They are typically spayed, microchipped, and up to date on shots.

Adopt a Beagle

Tuxedo Cat

Cat · Playful · Gets along with other cats

Tuxedo cats are energetic, charming, and full of personality. They love interactive toys and get along well with other pets and people.

Adopt a Tuxedo Cat

Labrador Retriever

Dog · Calm · Perfect for seniors & families

Labradors are calm, well-trained, and adaptable. They love slow walks and thrive in quiet homes as well as active families.

Adopt a Labrador

Grey Shorthair

Cat · Gentle · Best in quiet homes

Grey Shorthairs are calm, sweet, and deeply loyal once they trust you. They prefer peaceful spaces and will become your most devoted companion.

Adopt a Grey Shorthair
