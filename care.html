Pet Care Tips

Bringing a pet home is the start of something special. Here's how to set them up for success.

🍖

Feeding

Feed your pet at the same times each day. Use vet-recommended food portions and always keep fresh, clean water available.

🏃

Exercise & Play

Dogs need at least one walk a day. Cats need 15–20 minutes of interactive play. Movement keeps them happy and healthy.

🩺

Vet Visits

Book a vet checkup within the first week. Keep up with vaccinations, flea prevention, and yearly wellness exams.

❤️

Building Trust

New pets need time to settle in. Give them a quiet space, be patient, and let them come to you — trust grows slowly and lasts forever.

🎓

Training

Use positive reinforcement — treats, praise, and play. Reward the behavior you want. Never punish or yell. Short, daily sessions work best.

🛏️

A Safe Space

Set up a cozy bed or crate that belongs just to your pet. A safe, calm corner helps them feel secure in their new home.
