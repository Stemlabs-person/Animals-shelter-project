Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using FurAndFound — from adopting a pet to reporting a lost one.

🐾 General
FurAndFound is a community website that helps connect shelter animals with loving homes. We link to trusted organisations like Petfinder, the ASPCA, and the Humane Society where you can adopt, volunteer, or donate.

📋 Adoption
Head to our How It Works page for a full step-by-step guide. In short: search on Petfinder, contact the shelter, submit an application, and bring your new pet home.
Adoption fees typically range from $0 to $500 depending on the shelter and animal. The fee usually covers vaccinations, microchipping, and spay/neuter surgery — far less than buying from a breeder.
Yes, but you'll need your landlord's permission and may need to provide their contact details on your adoption application. Check your lease first — some buildings have pet size or breed restrictions.
🔍 Lost Pets
Go to our Lost Pet page, fill in your pet's details, upload a photo, and click Generate Poster. You can then print it or share it across social media, WhatsApp, SMS, and more.
The Lost Board is a community page where lost pet posters are displayed. After generating your poster, click "Post to Board" to add it. When your pet is found, click 🎉 Found! to remove it.
Currently posts are saved locally in your browser, so each visitor only sees their own. We're working on connecting a shared database so everyone sees the same board. Stay tuned!
🌿 Pet Care
Our Pet Care page covers feeding, exercise, vet visits, training, and building trust with a new pet. Each page also has a Further Reading section linking to expert guides from the ASPCA, Humane Society, and AVMA.
