How It Works

Here's exactly how Petfinder's adoption process works — from your first search to bringing your pet home.

1

Search & Save

Filter pets on Petfinder by species, breed, age, size, and location. Save your search and Petfinder will email you when new matches appear.

2

Contact the Shelter

Reach out directly to the shelter or rescue through Petfinder. Ask about the pet's temperament, medical history, and their specific adoption requirements.

3

Submit Your Application

Fill out the shelter's adoption application. You'll share details about your household, housing situation (landlord info if renting), other pets, and veterinary and personal references.

4

Review & Home Visit

The shelter reviews your application — this can take a few days to a few weeks. Some rescues require a home visit to confirm the space is safe and suitable for the pet.

5

Meet Your Pet

Once approved, you arrange a time to meet your pet in person — at the shelter or with the foster carer. This is your chance to confirm the match before committing.

6

Finalise & Go Home

Sign the adoption contract, pay the adoption fee (typically $0–$500, covering vaccinations, microchip, and spay/neuter), pick up your pet's records, and welcome them home.

Ready to take the first step?

Every pet on Petfinder is waiting — not for a perfect home, just for a loving one. Start your search, submit your application, and make a difference today.
